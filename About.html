About SPARSH Outreach Program

The SPARSH Outreach Program is an initiative committed to ensuring transparency, efficiency, and support in pension disbursement for our esteemed defence pensioners. Our goal is to bridge the gap between pensioners and authorities through dedicated assistance, awareness, and grievance redressal.

We strive to provide seamless access to services, address concerns on-the-spot, and empower pensioners with the necessary knowledge regarding the SPARSH system. Whether it’s grievance handling, PPO clarifications, or documentation support, our team is here to assist every step of the way.

This platform is a tribute to our veterans and their families. Through compassion, clarity, and commitment, we aim to honor their service by delivering what they truly deserve — care with dignity.
